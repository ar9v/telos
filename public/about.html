About Telos

Need

As college students and pomodoro users we asked ourselves this question: how do we know when we've disproportionately destined pomodoros to one school course over others? Telos is a possible answer to this problem. As per Parkinson's Law, tasks tend to take up as much time as they have for their completion, so as an artificial limit, we've decided to put pomodoros in the context of a week.

Inspiration

Telos is based on Francesco Cirillo's Pomodoro Technique and aims to be a step towards its goals beyond being a way to concentrate. Furthermore, Telos is inspired by the Time Travelers We believe that time management is a very personal endeavor, and that simplicity is paramount to success.
